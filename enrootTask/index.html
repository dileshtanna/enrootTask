Enroot Mumbai is a group of passionate and motivated individuals who wish to learn, build and share their knowledge and apply it for development of Mumbai

Why we are on this mission ?

We live in the Economic capital of India and yet when it comes to Quality of life we rank very low. Although we say that we live in the city of dreams we can rarely sleep peacefully. Stress has got all of us down and we don't even have the time to take a moment to realize that.

So we are on this mission to improve the quality of life and solve the social problems using sustainable technology, and we know that we are not alone, if you think you can help us in any form just send us a simple mail.


VISION

To solve problems and inculcate an attitude of problem solving and development amongst the citizens of Mumbai with collaborative support of each other.

MISSION

To improve the Quality of life of all the citizens of the city.


To ensure ecological and sustainable development of the city.


To find problems faced by the common citizens in their daily life and tackle them.


To encourage and harness young talent to form a community based approach.


VOLUNTEERING OPPORTUNITIES

Apply now to be part of our team !!!

Each one of us wishes to make a difference but alone we can't do much. Let's join our hands together in the pursuit of happiness. Rome wasn't built in a day and neither by a single person, let's all come together and create wonders.

We are welcoming everyone, whether you are a student, a working professional or a self-employed entrepreneur, if you think you can bring the change then yes you are right you can. A shoutout to all the Mumbaikars lets unite on a war against our problems.

PROCESS OF THE PROJECTS

This process will be monitored by our best project managers to ensure quality deployment of the projects.

Ideate

Identify the problem.


Research about cause of that problem.


Think of a solution.


Prototype

Wireframing.


Designing.


Feedback from stakeholders.


Build

Update the design.


Build the model.


Test the model.


Deploy

Launch.


Reach the audience.


Continue to update & improvise.


PROJECT DOMAINS

We accept problem statements and projects from the following domains.

Health & Safety

Solving problems in the field of Health and safety will help us live Safe, Healthy and Quality life in our City.

Mobility

Whether we are travelling to go to Work, College, or just a picnic we all face problems in reaching our destinations.

Environment

Mumbai is now a forest, but made of concrete, bricks and cement instead of trees and plants.

Employment

Each and every individual has a purpose to fulfill, its all about giving them the right opportunity to work.
